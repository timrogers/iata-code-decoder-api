Shetland Wind

Soft as kelp and morning light,
A Sheltie dances on the lea;
Eyes like twilight, sharp and bright,
Footfalls crisp as winter’s sea.

Echoes of the northern moor
Nestle in the sable fur;
Loyal heart that seeks the door,
Gentle guard who will not err.

Mirth in every prancing stride,
Wisdom tucked behind a grin;
Shepherd’s wit and ocean’s tide
Guide the kindly soul within.

By the hearth or on the hill,
Temper small but courage grand;
Shetland breeze, forever still,
Folds your world within its hand.

— For Shetland Sheepdogs, who keep our days bright and our laps warmer.
